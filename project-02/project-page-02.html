People at the beach!
A place where everyone looks happy and content. Rested and happy. To get to the beach, people leave their countries for a while, take vacations at work, get up early. And all in order to sit, swim and relax. It is where whole families gather at last, where everything is so simple and beautiful, where there are lots of smiles and meditation in the sun. Isn't that collective happiness?
A series of quick sketches of people on the beach while resting.
Ink, oil pastels, liner, 2023.
